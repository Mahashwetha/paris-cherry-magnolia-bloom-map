# 🌸 Paris Cherry Blossom & Magnolia Map

An interactive map of all 20 Paris arrondissements showing cherry blossom 🌸 and magnolia ✿ bloom spots, timings, and tips.

**Live site →** https://paris-cherry-magnolia-bloom-map.netlify.app/

## Features
- 🗺️ Hex grid map of Paris — click any arrondissement for details
- 🌸 Cherry blossom & ✿ magnolia spots per district
- 📍 Tap any park to open directly in Google Maps
- 🇬🇧 / 🇫🇷 English & French language toggle
- 🗓️ Bloom calendar (March → April)
- 🌸 Falling petals animation

## Bloom Season

| Period | What's blooming |
|--------|----------------|
| Early March | Magnolias open (5e, 16e, 20e) |
| Mid March | Magnolia peak (8e, 6e, 7e) |
| Late Mar – Early Apr ⭐ | Cherry blossom peak (12e, 16e, 8e) |
| Mid–Late April | Last blooms, Bagatelle festival (16e) |

## Top Spots
- 🏆 **Bois de Vincennes** (12e) — biggest cherry blossom destination in Paris
- 🌸 **Parc Monceau** (8e) — most famous Right Bank cherry spot
- ✿ **Jardin des Plantes** (5e) — legendary magnolia alley
- 🌸 **Parc de Bagatelle** (16e) — annual Hanami festival every April
